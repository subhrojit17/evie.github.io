Evie Frye

Overview

Dame Evie Frye (born 1847) was a Master Assassin of the British Brotherhood, active in London during the Victorian era, and the elder twin sister of Jacob Frye. She was also a member of the Order of the Sacred Garter, and the grand-aunt of Lydia Frye.

Originating from Crawley, Evie and her brother travelled to London to liberate London from the Templars' control and help the poverty-stricken masses, following the death of their father by natural causes. To accomplish this, she and Jacob founded the Rooks, a criminal syndicate that opposed the Templar-backed Blighters.

Special Abilities

1. Stealth
Widely regarded for her stealthy attributes, Evie excelled at masking her presence, to such an extent that guards were unable to notice her as long as she remained still.

2. Hidden Blade
The iconic blade of the Assassin Brotherhood. Evie was a Master Assassin proficient with the Hidden Blade.

3. Parkour and Freerunning
Another staple attribute of the Assassins, Evie was a fluent freerunner and excelled in parkour atop the high buildings in London paired with her trusty rope-launcher.

4. Rope-Launcher
A present from the acclaimed Alexander Graham Bell, the rope Launcher was a unique trademark weapon used by the Frye twins : Jacob and Evie during their conquests in London enabling them to scale tall buildings at ease.

5. Eagle Vision
An ability unique to all Assassins, Eagle Vision allows the person to summon the sight of an Eagle enabling sharp visual senses and the ability to percieve enemies through walls and tag their movements.

Evie, as a character is first introduced in Assassin's Creed : Syndicate in 2015 and is undoubtedly one the most visually pleasing characters to be created as part of the Assassin Universe. She is voiced by English actress Victoria Atkin.
